# Hydra's Breath

> [2026-05-28]: NEW 7.41 ITEM -- Builds from Dragon Lance + Specialist's Array.

> [2026-05-28]: STATS -- Provides bonus attack range.

> [2026-05-28]: EFFECT (DOT) -- Attacks apply a damage-over-time debuff dealing 2.5% of the target's maximum health as damage per second. Scales with enemy HP -- particularly effective against tanky/high-health heroes (strength carries, offlaners with high HP pools).

> [2026-05-28]: EFFECT (SPLIT SHOT) -- 30% chance on ranged attack to split and hit 3 targets in a 30 degree cone in front of the attacking hero. Provides farming efficiency and AOE right-click damage in fights when multiple heroes are grouped.

> [2026-05-28]: BUILD PATH CHOICE -- Dragon Lance upgrades into Hydra's Breath or Hurricane Pike. Hydra's Breath is preferred when the enemy has high-HP heroes where the % health DoT deals significant damage, or when AOE right-click value is high (grouped enemies, farming speed). Hurricane Pike is preferred when mobility or a push-away active is more valuable (kiting, repositioning, disengage).

> [2026-05-28]: HEROES -- Windranger (core upgrade option alongside Hurricane Pike).
