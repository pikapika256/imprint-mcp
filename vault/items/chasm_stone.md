# Chasm Stone

## Type
Utility — AOE Amplifier (component item)

## Mechanic
> [2026-05-27]: Provides +40 AOE bonus to spells. Increases the area of effect of the carrier's abilities.

## Upgrade Path
> [2026-05-27]: Builds into Shiva's Guard OR Gleipnir. Both are teamfight-oriented AOE items — the Chasm Stone upgrade path is fully committed to AOE/teamfight value.

## Core on heroes
> [2026-05-27]: Core on Hoodwink — increases Bushwhack AOE, making it significantly easier to catch multiple heroes.
> [2026-05-27]: Strong on any hero with AOE spells in a teamfight role. Worth noting on other pos 3/4 heroes with AOE abilities.

## Matchup Impact
> [2026-05-27]: Teams with Chasm Stone carriers are harder to spread out against — AOE spells punish grouping more severely.
> [2026-05-27]: Draft consideration: if enemy has Chasm Stone on a key spellcaster, avoid 5-man grouping without BKB or magic resistance.

## Synergies
> [2026-05-27]: Hoodwink — Bushwhack AOE increase makes the stun zone larger and harder to dodge.
> [2026-05-27]: Leads into Shiva's Guard (armor aura + AOE slow) or Gleipnir (AOE root + magic damage) — both strong teamfight items.

## Notes
> [2026-05-27]: Despite Glimmer Cape and Solar Crest being weak this patch, the Chasm Stone upgrade path (Shiva's / Gleipnir) is very strong. Supports should favour this component over traditional armor/utility items.
