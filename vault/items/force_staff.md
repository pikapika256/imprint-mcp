## Notes

> [2026-05-27]: TIER — Strong meta item in 7.41c alongside Blink Dagger.
> [2026-05-27]: Core on pos 4 heroes — enables spell landing, repositioning, and escape across all top pos 4 picks.
> [2026-05-27]: Pairs naturally with heroes that need precise positioning to land key abilities.

> [2026-05-27]: RUBICK SYNERGY — Rubick's built-in cast range increase makes Force Staff especially powerful on him. He can reach allies at a greater distance than other supports, making him one of the best Force Staff carriers as a save tool this patch.

> [2026-05-27]: HOODWINK SYNERGY — Scurry allows item use during the movement, meaning Force Staff can be activated mid-Scurry. Higher effective usability than on other heroes — escape ceiling is significantly higher.

> [2026-05-28]: COUNTER TO AOE DOOM BUILD — Force Staff is the primary disengage tool against Doom's Aghanim's AOE build. Force Staff out of the AOE Doom zone, then BKB to prevent follow-up. Draft Force Staff access on supports when facing Doom.
