## Notes

> [2026-05-27]: ITEM META SHIFT — Heroes that utilise Blink Dagger and Force Staff to enable spells are dominant. Mobility items that let you close distance or reposition to land abilities are core to the current meta.
> [2026-05-27]: WEAK ITEMS — Glimmer Cape and Solar Crest are undervalued / below meta-strength this patch. Teams investing in these items are likely getting lower value than alternatives.
> [2026-05-27]: POS 4 META — The position 4 (soft support / roamer) is highly influential this patch. Top 5 pos 4 heroes: Phoenix, Rubick, Clockwerk, Techies, Hoodwink.

> [2026-05-27]: RUBICK META NOTE — Being played primarily as a save support (Aghanims Shard + Force Staff) rather than a spell-stealer. Built-in cast range amplifies the Force Staff save reach.
> [2026-05-27]: PHOENIX DRAFT NOTE — Requires draft-level answers. Easier to ban than to counter in-game. Lane phase vulnerability: long early CDs, but dangerous when spells are available.

> [2026-05-27]: TECHIES PRE-BKB WINDOW — Reactive Taser disarm very strong before enemy core BKB. Fights should be sought in this window.
> [2026-05-27]: HOODWINK — Chasm Stone + upgrades synergy with Bushwhack AOE stun is a notable power spike this patch. Wave push income makes her a semi-carry pos 4.
> [2026-05-27]: CLOCKWERK — Space creation and vision role in fights rather than pure initiation.

> [2026-05-27]: CHASM STONE — New item (+40 AOE to spells). Builds into Shiva's Guard or Gleipnir. Core on Hoodwink and other AOE spellcasters. Despite Glimmer / Solar Crest being weak, this upgrade path is strong.
> [2026-05-27]: GLEIPNIR PATH — Chasm Stone into Gleipnir is a strong support item trajectory this patch, especially on heroes with AOE stuns (Hoodwink Bushwhack).

> [2026-05-28]: CORRECTION — Doom does NOT counter BKB-reliant lineups. Doom ultimate is blocked by BKB.

> [2026-05-28]: DOOM PROFILE — Strengths: survivability, fast farm, strong lane, late game scale. Weakness: item-reliant, weak mid-game window. Silences + prevents healing. Aghanims AOE build + Shiva's + Scorched Earth speed is a potent late-game pattern. Countered by Linken's (single), ranged high-damage carries (Windranger), Force Staff+BKB vs AOE build.
> [2026-05-28]: GLEIPNIR CONFIRMED — Does not go through BKB. BKB = full counter. Linken's = single-target counter.

> [2026-05-28]: POS 5 TIER: Rubick, Techies, Clockwerk (flex 4/5), Lich, Bane.
> [2026-05-28]: SUPPORT FLEX META — Rubick, Techies, Clockwerk create draft ambiguity by being viable at both pos 4 and pos 5. Opponents cannot reliably read lane assignments.
