# Monkey King

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Primary position 1 carry. Can flex to mid lane or rarely position 4.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Fast-paced lane bully. Very strong against melee heroes in lane. High map mobility enables constant threat presence across the map. Strong teamfight ultimate (Wukong's Command) creates a large AOE zone -- pairs well with AOE stun heroes that can lock enemies inside it.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Mage Slayer + Diffusal Blade into Desolator. Then BKB followed by standard melee carry items depending on the game.

> [2026-05-28]: SURVIVABILITY -- Lifesteal buff acquired from landing hits on enemy heroes (Jingu Mastery stacks). Survivability is dependent on being able to hit enemies -- interrupted by dispels or being unable to attack freely.

> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERED BY -- Dispels (removes Jingu Mastery lifesteal stacks, stripping his sustain). Vision (negates tree movement and ambush potential). Both effectively reduce his fighting capability significantly.

> [2026-05-28]: MID LANE FLEX -- Can be played mid to fill a lane bully role but is currently less dominant than previously. Patch changes reduced his strongest mid matchups from ~70-30 to closer to 55-45, and he is exposed to support ganks in the mid lane in a way that pos 1 avoids. Some teams still utilise this option. Item build is identical to the pos 1 build.

> [2026-05-28]: POS 4 FLEX -- Rarely played as position 4. Not a common pick in the current meta but the option exists.

> [2026-05-28]: PRO PLAYER -- 9class (Parivision) is a known specialist on Monkey King. If Parivision are in an upcoming tournament, Monkey King position 4 in particular is a realistic option from this player and worth factoring into draft preparation against that team.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 58.3% win rate on 24 picks at pos 1, 46 bans, 37.8% contest rate. Solid performer. Mid-tier ban priority.
