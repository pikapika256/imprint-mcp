# Axe

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 3 offlaner. Teamfight initiator with BKB-piercing disable.
> [2026-05-28]: PLAYER SPECIFIC -- Currently a player-specific pick. High win rate in DreamLeague S29 (59.5%) is partly attributable to certain players excelling on the hero. Not recommended as a general draft pick without strong player proficiency.

> [2026-05-28]: KIT -- Berserker's Call is a BKB-piercing AOE taunt. Forces nearby enemy heroes to attack Axe while he gains armour. This bypasses BKB entirely -- fragile position 1 heroes that rely on BKB to survive teamfights cannot escape being forced to attack Axe. Counter Helix provides passive AOE damage during the taunt window. Culling Blade is a BKB-piercing execute at a health threshold.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Vanguard first for early farming efficiency and durability. Then Blink Dagger (initiation), Consecrated Wraps (HP + magic resistance), BKB (survivability to enable diving in). Together these make Axe a durable teamfight initiator.

> [2026-05-28]: BKB PIERCING STRENGTH -- Berserker's Call goes through BKB, making Axe a strong counter to fragile position 1 heroes who rely on BKB to survive. Once BKB is active, these heroes normally become untouchable -- Axe's Call forces them to right-click him regardless, often burning their BKB with no benefit while Axe stacks Counter Helix spins.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Lanes well with ranged position 4 heroes. Battle Hunger combined with a ranged support enables favourable trades against position 5 heroes -- Battle Hunger forces the opponent to either take chip damage or fight, which the ranged pos 4 can exploit.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 59.5% win rate on 42 picks at pos 3, 36 bans, 42.2% contest rate. Strong performer in this tournament. Player-specific nature means win rate is elevated by hero specialists.
