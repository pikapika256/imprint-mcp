# Underlord

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 3 offlaner.
> [2026-05-28]: META SHIFT -- Historically played as an aura hero. Current trend in 7.41c is to play him as a teamfight control hero instead, leveraging his root abilities and Chasm Stone item synergy.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CURRENT META) -- Chasm Stone path into Gleipnir + Aghanim's Scepter + Aghanim's Shard. Gleipnir provides AOE root lockdown; Aghs + Shard upgrade his control abilities. Heavy root-focused build.

> [2026-05-28]: BKB MANDATORY (ENEMY) -- Multiple root abilities in the kit make BKB a mandatory purchase for enemy cores. Roots bypass BKB in some cases and the sheer volume of control forces BKB responses from the enemy lineup.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Stable laner but less survivable than the top pos 3 options (Timbersaw, Tidehunter). Has better matchups against Spirit heroes than Timbersaw specifically -- the damage profile and kit engage differently with mobile heroes. Less raw damage output than Timbersaw overall.

> [2026-05-28]: GLOBAL PRESENCE -- Dark Rift (ultimate) teleports Underlord and nearby allies to any allied unit globally. Allows him to farm away from the team safely and join fights globally on demand, similar in function to Doom or Invoker. Enables more farm than most pos 3 heroes without sacrificing fight participation, improving late-game scaling.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 50.0% win rate on 26 picks at pos 3, 58 bans, 45.4% contest rate. Even win rate at moderate volume. Ban rate reflects the teamfight control threat and BKB pressure the hero creates.
