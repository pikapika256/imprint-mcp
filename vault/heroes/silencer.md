# Silencer
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 5 support.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Strong against heroes weak to silences. Excels against lineups with save spells (Rubick, Oracle, Shadow Demon) and high-mobility heroes that rely on spells to escape. Global Silence removes all active abilities map-wide, creating a window where the enemy team cannot respond with spells.
> [2026-05-28]: DRAFT PRESSURE -- Forces enemies into sub-optimal item builds to deal with silences (e.g. Eul's Scepter for purge), diverting resources from power items.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Refresher Orb is the key late-game timing -- double Global Silence creates an extended no-spell window that can win teamfights outright.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Vulnerable to being jumped. No mobility or survivability in lane. If the enemy can find and kill Silencer quickly his value is severely reduced.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 50.0% win rate on 30 picks at pos 5, 31 bans. Even performance, moderate ban rate.
