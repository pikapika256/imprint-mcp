# Winter Wyvern
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 5 support.
> [2026-05-28]: COLD EMBRACE -- Hard counters right-click heroes. Encases the target in ice making them invulnerable to all damage except from their own allies. Forces enemies to stop attacking or damage their own team. Very strong in lane as a defensive tool and in fights against physical damage lineups.
> [2026-05-28]: WINTERS CURSE (ULTIMATE) -- BKB-piercing. Forces nearby enemies of the cursed hero to attack them. Counters BKB-reliant heroes as the disable goes through spell immunity.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Laning is weak overall and can be exploited with aggression. No reliable farming tools -- can lack items throughout the game, limiting late-game contribution to what base kit provides.
