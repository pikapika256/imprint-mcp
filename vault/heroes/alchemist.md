# Alchemist

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 1 carry. Hyper farmer.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Farms at exceptional speed to purchase Aghanim's Scepter and gift it to an ally, dramatically accelerating the team's power spike. The gift provides the ally's Aghs upgrade AND increases the recipient's farm speed. After gifting, Alchemist builds his own item core.

> [2026-05-28]: AGHS GIFT TARGETS (TOP) -- Queen of Pain, Ember Spirit, Pangolier, Primal Beast. These heroes benefit most from an early Aghs upgrade and the farm speed increase. Draft around these synergies when picking Alchemist.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Radiance (farming tool + passive damage), then farms Aghanim's Scepter for the gift. After gifting: Blink Dagger, BKB, Assault Cuirass. Then a control item -- Abyssal Blade (BKB-piercing lockdown) or Bloodthorn (silence + crit amplification).

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Not a strong laning hero. Can be punished in lane -- prioritising early aggression against Alchemist is a valid strategy to delay his farm timings.

> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERED BY -- Anti-regen (Ancient Apparition, Spirit Vessel) counters his high base regen. High burst damage can kill him before regen sustains him through the fight.

> [2026-05-28]: DRAFT PRIORITY -- Requires coordination to shut down. An Alchemist left uncontested for 15 minutes can complete Radiance, gift an Aghs, and have significant additional items. The enemy team must apply pressure early and often or accept being behind on net worth. Snowballs hard if given space.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 54.2% win rate on 24 picks at pos 1, 41 bans, 35.1% contest rate. Solid performer. Ban rate reflects the Aghs gift acceleration threat rather than Alchemist's own fighting strength.
