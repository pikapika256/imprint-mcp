> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only. Laning advantages snowball significantly.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (20-80), Viper (30-70): extremely difficult lanes. Sniper (35-65): outranged. Cheese tactic available for all three (see below).
> [2026-05-28]: UNFAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Snapfire (45-55): more raw damage in lane.
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Queen of Pain (50-50).
> [2026-05-28]: FAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Void Spirit (55-45), Puck (55-45), Invoker (55-45), Tiny (55-45), Pangolier (55-45), Primal Beast (55-45), Sand King (55-45), Earth Spirit (60-40).
> [2026-05-28]: LANING CHEESE -- Against hard counter matchups (Huskar, Viper, Sniper) and any matchup outside 40-60, Spirit heroes can drag creeps behind the enemy tower. This denies the enemy mid lane cs and pressure, effectively neutralising a bad lane matchup and making it closer to 50-50. Applicable to melee mid heroes generally -- not Spirit-specific. Use this as the standard response to unwinnable mid matchups rather than trying to fight the lane directly.


> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Provides low-commitment setup for follow-up damage from supports. Sleight of Fist combined with Searing Chains creates a reliable AOE lockdown pattern without requiring Ember to fully commit -- can disengage via Fire Remnant after the chain. High mobility allows him to dodge spells with skill and close gaps on priority targets.
> [2026-05-28]: SURVIVABILITY -- Flame Guard provides natural survivability against magic damage. Usually paired with Mage Slayer to further reduce incoming magic damage, making Ember very difficult to burst with spells.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Kaya and Sange is a core item. Provides spell amplification, HP, and status resistance.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (STANDARD) -- Aghanim's Scepter, Octarine Core, BKB, Refresher Orb form the backbone of the late-game build. BKB enables safe initiation; Refresher provides double fight presence; Octarine reduces cooldowns across the kit.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (SITUATIONAL) -- Manta Style or Eul's Scepter of Divinity against lineups with silences or roots (dispel or self-purge). Shiva's Guard against healing-focused lineups (applies healing reduction debuff). Hydra's Breath against high-HP cores -- Sleight of Fist hits multiple times per cast, applying Hydra's Breath's 2.5% max health DoT on each hit, making the item highly efficient when the target has a large HP pool.
