> [2026-05-27]: TIER — Top 5 pos 4 in 7.41c. High priority ban or pick.

> [2026-05-27]: LANING — Early cooldowns are very long. Heroes that can exploit this window (burst him before spells are back up) can bully the lane. Key: the laner must not be susceptible to Phoenix's damage when spells ARE available — avoid squishy laners that get punished by Fire Spirits + Sun Ray once CDs reset.
> [2026-05-27]: MID/LATE GAME — Requires specific draft-level answers to deal with effectively. Ad-hoc in-game countering is unreliable — you need the right heroes in your lineup before the game starts.
> [2026-05-27]: DRAFT PHILOSOPHY — Phoenix is a ban-or-draft-around hero. It is often easier to ban outright than to build a full counter-lineup, especially in best-of-1 formats. If not banned, your draft must include a reliable Supernova interrupter and heroes not weak to Fire Spirits.
> [2026-05-27]: COUNTER WINDOW — Target Phoenix in the lane phase by aggressing during his long early CD windows. Do not fight him when his spells are available.

> [2026-05-28]: CORRECTION — Phoenix does NOT utilise Blink Dagger. Icarus Dive provides built-in mobility for initiation and repositioning — an external Blink is redundant.
> [2026-05-28]: Phoenix is strong in the top pos 4 tier for reasons OTHER than the Blink/Force Staff item meta — his kit is self-sufficient for mobility. His strength comes from Fire Spirits damage, Supernova teamfight impact, and Sun Ray sustain/damage.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD — Spirit Vessel is the core first item. Provides healing reduction (fits Phoenix's anti-healing theme), HP, and damage over time.
> [2026-05-28]: SECOND ITEM — Eul's Scepter of Divinity if dispel is needed (e.g. vs heavy hex/silence lineups or to self-dispel for Supernova setup).
> [2026-05-28]: UTILITY ITEMS — Lotus Orb or Scythe of Vyse (Hex) after core, depending on what the team needs.
> [2026-05-28]: LATE GAME SCALING — Shiva's Guard (AOE slow, fits teamfight), Refresher Orb (double Supernova — extremely high teamfight value), or Aghanim's Scepter for further upgrade.
> [2026-05-28]: REFRESHER PATTERN — Phoenix + Refresher Orb in late game = double Supernova. Very high teamfight value, hard to deal with without multiple Supernova counters available.
