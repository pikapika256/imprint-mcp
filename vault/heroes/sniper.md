> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only. Sniper is one of the strongest laning ranged mids -- dominates most melee heroes and counters Huskar.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTER (LANE) -- Huskar (65-35 sniper): one of the few ranged heroes that can safely lane against Huskar.
> [2026-05-28]: DOMINATES (LANE) -- Viper (65-35), Tiny (65-35): clearly favoured. Sand King (60-40): favoured. All melee Spirit heroes fall heavily against Sniper.
> [2026-05-28]: SLIGHT DISADVANTAGE (LANE) -- Pangolier (45-55): Pangolier slightly favoured.
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Primal Beast (50-50).

> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Carry hero in the mid lane due to heavy level dependence. Not a traditional mid in terms of map activity -- passive playstyle focused on farming and scaling rather than roaming or making early moves.
> [2026-05-28]: MOBILITY -- No built-in mobility spells. Highly vulnerable to gap-closing heroes that can get on top of him. Compensates with extreme attack range, forcing enemies to cross a large distance before engaging.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Farm-focused mid game. Uses attack range to safely deal damage from the backline without needing to commit to fights. Not an active roamer or objective taker in the early-mid game.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (STANDARD) -- Hydra's Breath (attack range extension + % health DoT + split shot), BKB (survivability and safe damage delivery), Shadow Blade (provides escape and surprise initiation to offset the lack of built-in mobility). Shadow Blade allows Sniper to reposition or disengage in emergencies.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE) -- After core, build right-click items that increase damage output or survivability. Examples: Butterfly (evasion + attack speed), Daedalus (critical strike damage), Satanic (lifesteal + sustain in fights). Combination of these three is a standard late-game finishing build.
