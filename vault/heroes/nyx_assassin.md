# Nyx Assassin
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 4 support.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Invisibility-based setup hero. Uses permanent invisibility (Vendetta) to position for team fights and pick-offs without being seen. Sets up kills rather than dealing damage in a conventional sense.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Utility-focused: Blink Dagger, Force Staff, Eul's Scepter. Items enable initiation and save from the invisible position.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAK LANING -- Very weak laner. Often abuses creep pulling to reduce time in a losing lane. Similar in concept to the mid lane wave cut strategy -- minimises lane disadvantage rather than winning the lane.
> [2026-05-28]: DISADVANTAGEOUS LANE FLEX -- Frequently used by pos 4 players when the lane matchup is unfavourable. Pulling creeps and roaming early converts a losing lane into a neutral one. Worth flagging in drafts where the pos 4 matchup is one-sided -- Nyx allows the player to sidestep the lane entirely.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 64.3% win rate on 14 picks, 20 bans. Strong performer at low volume. Low ban rate relative to win rate -- potentially undervalued.
