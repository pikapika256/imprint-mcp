# Grimstroke

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Primarily position 4. Can also be played position 5.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Strong single-target control hero. Silence, Ink Swell, and Soulbind ultimate provide high disabling value.

> [2026-05-28]: INK SWELL -- Wraps a target (ally or enemy) in ink, slowing and dealing damage on expiry. Combos well with melee heroes who stick on a target -- the wrapped hero takes increased damage and cannot act freely, amplifying the melee hero's sustained right-click window.
> [2026-05-28]: SOULBIND (ULTIMATE) -- BKB-piercing leash that links two enemy heroes together. Spells affecting one linked hero affect the other. Hard counter to Pangolier (Rolling Thunder becomes impossible to use without hitting the linked ally) and highly mobile Spirit heroes (pins them in place regardless of BKB).

> [2026-05-28]: DRAFT NOTE -- High priority consideration against Pangolier and Spirit heroes (Storm, Ember, Void) due to Soulbind being BKB-piercing. Also pairs well with any melee hero that needs setup to stick on a target (e.g. Dawnbreaker, Axe, Beastmaster).

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 66.7% win rate on 18 picks at pos 5, 5 bans. Very low ban rate relative to performance -- potentially undervalued in the draft phase. Strong performer when picked.
