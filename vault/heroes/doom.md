> [2026-05-27]: TIER — Top pos 3 offlaner in 7.41c.
> [2026-05-27]: Priority pick/ban when BKB-reliant carries are in the enemy draft.

> [2026-05-28]: CORRECTION — Doom ultimate does NOT bypass BKB. Doom is not an answer to BKB-reliant lineups.
> [2026-05-28]: Doom strength in 7.41c is for other reasons — Devour neutral ability access, raw lane presence, and catch potential rather than BKB bypass.

> [2026-05-28]: STRENGTHS — High survivability, fast farming, strong laning phase, strong late game scaling. A hero that gets stronger the longer the game goes.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS — Item-reliant to be relevant. Can be weak in the mid-game window before items come online. Opponents should look to take advantages and force fights in this window.
> [2026-05-28]: ABILITY — Doom silences AND prevents healing on the target. Counters heroes that rely on mobility items/spells or healing (Lifestealer, Dazzle saves, Omniknight, etc). Strong vs healing-focused lineups.
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTER — Linken's Sphere blocks Doom on a single target. Primary item counter.
> [2026-05-28]: AGHANIMS BUILD — Aghanim's Scepter upgrade allows Doom to self-cast, dealing AOE Doom around himself. When combined with Shiva's Guard (+AOE from Chasm Stone component) and high movement speed from Scorched Earth, this becomes a potent teamfight pattern — enemy team has to disengage or get caught in the AOE.
> [2026-05-28]: AGHANIMS COUNTER — Force Staff + BKB to disengage from AOE Doom. Force Staff out of range, BKB to resist the zone while repositioning.
> [2026-05-28]: GENERAL COUNTER — Ranged heroes with high damage output (e.g. Windranger) counter Doom. They can punish him from range before he closes the gap with Scorched Earth movement speed.
> [2026-05-28]: MID-GAME WINDOW -- Doom is most vulnerable in the 20-30 minute window before Shiva's Guard + Aghanim's Scepter come online. In this window Doom lacks the AOE threat of the Aghs self-cast and has lower tankiness without Shiva's armour. Enemies should prioritise forcing teamfights, taking Roshan, and pushing high ground before these items are completed.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BENCHMARK -- Once Doom has Shiva's Guard component (Chasm Stone) + Aghs the threat level increases significantly. Approximately 30-35 min mark in high-level play depending on farm efficiency. Punish window is roughly minutes 18-30.
