# Beastmaster

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Flex hero across pos 1, 2, and 3.
> [2026-05-28]: KEY SPELL -- Primal Roar is a BKB-piercing single-target stun. Cannot be blocked by BKB. This spell dictates both the hero's value and his counters -- Linken's Sphere blocks Primal Roar on the protected target, making it the primary counter item.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (POS 3) -- Helm of the Overlord (Helm of Dominator upgrade), Assault Cuirass, and aura items. Focused on pressuring objectives early with strong timing -- dominated push timings using the Wild Boar + Hawk for vision and split push pressure. Aura items amplify the whole team in objective fights.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (POS 1/2) -- Aghanim's Scepter, Blink Dagger, BKB. Focus on farming quickly and generating high damage output. Playstyle is to build net worth, then use Primal Roar BKB-piercing stun to pick off targets and kill within the control window.

> [2026-05-28]: LINKEN'S COUNTER -- Linken's Sphere blocks Primal Roar on a single target. Primary counter item when facing Beastmaster -- key heroes on the enemy team should consider Linken's as a priority purchase.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 44.0% win rate on 25 picks, 76 bans, 54.6% contest rate at pos 3. High ban rate reflects the flex threat (forces draft read on role) and the BKB-piercing Roar value. Win rate is moderate when picked.
