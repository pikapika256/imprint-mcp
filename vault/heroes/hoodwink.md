> [2026-05-27]: High mobility ceiling in current meta — mobility items amplify her kit significantly.

> [2026-05-27]: SCURRY MECHANIC — Scurry allows item activation during movement. This means Force Staff can be used mid-Scurry, making her escape and repositioning ceiling significantly higher than other Force Staff carriers. Mechanical advantage over other pos 4 Force Staff users.
> [2026-05-27]: AOE STUN STRENGTH — Bushwhack AOE stun is very strong in current meta, especially combined with Chasm Stone item and its upgrades (increases stun effectiveness / area).
> [2026-05-27]: WAVE PUSH UTILITY — Can safely push waves throughout the game using spells + Scurry mobility. This earns more net worth than typical pos 4 heroes, accelerating item timings. Treat Hoodwink as a semi-carry pos 4 in terms of farm expectation.
> [2026-05-27]: ITEM TRAJECTORY — Earlier Force Staff + Chasm Stone timings than comparable heroes due to wave push income.
> [2026-05-27]: DRAFT VALUE — Brings stun, wave clear, safe farming, and Force Staff synergy in one hero. Very low-risk pick for the draft.

> [2026-05-27]: ITEM PATH — Chasm Stone core first item. Upgrades into Gleipnir (lockdown chain with Bushwhack) or Shiva's Guard (AOE slow for teamfights). Force Staff provides mobility during Scurry. This trio (Force Staff + Chasm Stone + upgrade) is the standard pos 4 Hoodwink build.
> [2026-05-27]: STUN CHAIN — Bushwhack (larger AOE from Chasm Stone) into Gleipnir root = extended lockdown window. Very difficult for enemy to survive without Linken's or BKB.
