> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (20-80): unplayable. Viper (35-65): highly unfavourable. Sniper (40-60): outranged. Creep drag viable for all three.
> [2026-05-28]: UNFAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Queen of Pain (45-55), Snapfire (40-60).
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Earth Spirit (50-50), Tiny (50-50), Pangolier (50-50).
> [2026-05-28]: FAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Puck (55-45), Invoker (55-45), Primal Beast (55-45), Sand King (55-45).
> [2026-05-28]: LANING CHEESE -- Melee hero. Creep drag behind enemy tower is the standard response to hard counter matchups (Huskar, Viper, Sniper).

> [2026-05-28]: WAVE CUT (TIER 2) -- Can execute wave cutting behind the enemy tower in unfavourable matchups, but less effectively than Sand King/Primal Beast. Typically improves a bad matchup to around 40-60 rather than 50-50. Still valuable vs hard counters like Huskar and Viper.

> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Lowest damage output of the three Spirit heroes. Compensates with the highest survivability: Dissimilate provides an all-damage barrier and two mobility spells give him consistent escape and repositioning options. Harder to kill than Storm or Ember but deals less impact damage.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Low-commitment poke and setup hero, similar to Ember Spirit. Prods and harasses to create openings for his team rather than looking for direct kills. Does not need to fully commit to a fight to provide value.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Mirrors Ember Spirit's build (Kaya and Sange, Aghanim's Scepter, Octarine Core, BKB, Refresher, situational items). Key difference: Aghanim's Shard is purchased early as a priority item -- it significantly improves his farming efficiency and enables him to clear waves and jungle faster, allowing him to keep pace with the rest of the build.
> [2026-05-28]: SURVIVABILITY -- Most survivable Spirit hero. Dissimilate barrier absorbs all damage types. Combined with two mobility spells, Void Spirit is very difficult to kill when played correctly.
