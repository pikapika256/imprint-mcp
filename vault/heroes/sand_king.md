> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (20-80), Viper (30-70): near-unplayable. Sniper (40-60), Queen of Pain (35-65): heavily unfavourable. Creep drag applicable (melee hero).

> [2026-05-28]: FAVOURABLE (LANE) -- None clearly favourable in this pool.

> [2026-05-28]: WAVE CUT (TIER 1) -- Sand King and Primal Beast are the primary wave-cutting melee mids. Strong AOE damage clears the wave behind the enemy tower efficiently, converting even 20-80 matchups (Huskar/Viper) into approximately 50-50 or better.

> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played as position 3 offlaner with the same build and playstyle.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Disruptive teamfighter. Uses Burrowstrike + Epicenter to initiate and deal massive AOE damage in fights.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Blink Dagger, Shiva's Guard, Bloodstone. Blink enables initiation; Shiva's adds AOE slow and armour; Bloodstone provides mana sustain and spell lifesteal to survive in the middle of fights.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CONTROL) -- Aghanim's Scepter provides strong damage amplification and combines well with Abyssal Blade or Basher. This combination provides BKB-piercing lockdown, which directly addresses Sand King's primary weakness to BKB.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (BKB) -- BKB is required at some point to enable safe initiation and survive in the middle of enemy teams.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE) -- Refresher Orb is a strong late-game item -- double Epicenter provides enormous teamfight damage and control. Other considerations: Octarine Core (cooldown reduction), Kaya and Sange (spell amp + HP).
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Countered by BKB. Epicenter is magic damage and blocked by spell immunity, significantly reducing teamfight output. Aghanim's Scepter + Abyssal Blade/Basher directly mitigates this by providing BKB-piercing damage and lockdown to punish enemies even when BKB is active.
