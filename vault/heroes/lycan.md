# Lycan
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 3 offlaner (primarily).
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Summons hero. Relies on Helm of the Overlord to control a powerful neutral creep, using it to safely secure farm and amplify fighting capability. Very strong against heroes without mobility -- wolves and the controlled creep can run down immobile targets and punish weak supports caught out of position.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE TIMING) -- Helm of the Overlord into Assault Cuirass. Once both are complete Lycan pushes objectives very easily -- wolves + controlled creep + AC provides a powerful push timing with global pressure.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LOCKDOWN) -- No built-in lockdown. Builds Hex and/or Abyssal Blade to create lockdown on targets. Nullifier is usually required to deal with Ghost Sceptre and Eul's Scepter purchases by enemies trying to avoid being run down.
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTER ITEM -- Ghost Sceptre is a must-purchase for heroes susceptible to being run down by Lycan. Provides temporary invulnerability to physical damage, breaking his attack chain.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 41.7% win rate on 12 picks, 40 bans. High ban rate reflects the objective timing threat more than combat dominance.
