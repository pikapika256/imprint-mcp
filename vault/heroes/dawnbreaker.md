> [2026-05-27]: TIER — Top pos 3 offlaner in 7.41c.
> [2026-05-27]: Global Solar Guardian provides cross-map teamfight presence. Blink Dagger + Solar Guardian is core combo.
> [2026-05-27]: High base damage with mobility — fits Blink/Force Staff enabling meta.
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Pos 3 offlaner. Survivable right-click hero with global presence via Solar Guardian.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Two AOE damage and mobility spells allow Dawnbreaker to either start aggression or disengage from pressure while maintaining CS. Difficult to zone out of lane.

> [2026-05-28]: GLOBAL FARMING -- After laning phase, Dawnbreaker can farm away from the team and use Solar Guardian to join fights globally. This allows her to farm significantly more than most pos 3 heroes without sacrificing fight participation. A major strength of the hero in terms of net worth relative to role.

> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Plays as a survivable right-click hero. Primary damage comes from attack damage amplified by items, supplemented by her innate built-in heal. Not a pure caster -- item build reflects this physical damage identity.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Harpoon + BKB is the core. Harpoon (Dragon Lance + Echo Sabre upgrade) pulls the target in, allowing Dawnbreaker to stay on them. BKB provides spell immunity to survive in the middle of the fight while right-clicking.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (DAMAGE/STAY ON TARGET) -- Assault Cuirass amplifies right-click damage and provides armour survivability. Abyssal Blade provides further lockdown to keep targets in range and unable to escape. Both items reinforce the right-click carry identity.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (AURA -- RARELY PLAYED) -- An alternative aura-focused build exists using Consecrated Wraps, Aghanim's Scepter, Crimson Guard, Pipe of Insight, and Greaves. This playstyle is rarely viable in the current patch due to the general weakness of aura and defensive items in 7.41c. Not recommended in most drafts.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Counters ranged damage dealers that she can close the gap on and stick to. Shadow Fiend and Drow Ranger are examples -- heroes that deal damage from range but are vulnerable when a melee hero gets on top of them. Dawnbreaker's mobility and Harpoon make it difficult for them to disengage.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (BAD) -- Countered by stronger right-click heroes that out-stat her in a direct fight (e.g. Ursa). Also countered by high-mobility heroes that can disengage and avoid being stuck in her range -- negating her ability to stay on target which is her primary fighting pattern.
