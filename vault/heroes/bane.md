# Bane

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 5 support. (See also pos 4 flex in some matchups.)
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Lane bully and BKB-piercing control hero. Fiend's Grip is a long-duration channelled stun that pierces BKB -- one of the most powerful disables in the game in the mid and late game window.

> [2026-05-28]: LANE BULLYING -- Brain Sap provides strong HP trading in lane -- deals damage and heals Bane simultaneously. Trades very favourably in lane, especially against heroes without reliable burst. Nightmare enables kill setups or disengages -- can put an enemy to sleep to prevent escape, or an ally to interrupt enemy channelling spells.

> [2026-05-28]: FIEND'S GRIP -- Long duration BKB-piercing channel stun. Primary late-game value. Sets up kills with reliable lockdown regardless of BKB. Key weakness: channelled ability -- any damage to Bane while channelling interrupts it. Heroes and items that deal damage to Bane cancel the grip.

> [2026-05-28]: NIGHTMARE -- Powerful save: puts an ally to sleep making them untargetable by most spells. Also used offensively for kill setup or disengaging from fights.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Builds mobility items due to low natural mobility. Specific items vary -- Force Staff, Blink Dagger, Eul's Scepter are all viable depending on what mobility/utility is needed.

> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Countered by heroes that can deal damage to Bane while he channels Fiend's Grip -- interrupting the channel removes his primary value. AOE damage, summons, or any hero that can quickly hit Bane during the channel are effective counters.

> [2026-05-28]: PHOENIX COUNTER -- Confirmed hard counter to Phoenix in lane (see bane_vs_phoenix.md).

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 68.8% win rate on 16 picks at pos 5, 2 bans. Very high win rate at very low ban rate -- potentially significantly undervalued as a ban target given the performance. Worth monitoring as a sleeper pick.
