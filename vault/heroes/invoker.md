> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Generally passive post-laning. Invoker scales extremely strongly with levels and items, so the priority is reaching key item benchmarks safely rather than making aggressive moves. Forge Spirits with Boots of Travel enables safe split pushing -- can apply lane pressure globally without risk.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Meteor Hammer is a core early item. Provides mana sustain and combos directly with Tornado + Cold Snap setup: Tornado lifts the target, Meteor Hammer activates during the airborne window, Cold Snap keeps them locked when they land.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (MOBILITY) -- Boots of Travel purchased to enable safe global split pushing with Forge Spirits. Allows Invoker to apply map pressure while remaining able to teleport into fights.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (MID GAME) -- Chasm Stone component item (path to Shiva's Guard), BKB, and Hex form the mid-game fighting core. BKB enables safe spell delivery; Hex provides hard lockdown to land the full spell combo on a target; Shiva's Guard adds AOE slow and armour.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE) -- After core, flexible options: Octarine Core (cooldown reduction + spell lifesteal), Aghanim's Scepter (spell upgrades), Refresher Orb (double combo), Linken's Sphere (survivability vs single-target control).
> [2026-05-28]: SCALING -- One of the strongest scaling heroes in the pool. Given time to reach late-game item benchmarks, Invoker's spell combinations deal enormous damage. Early passivity is intentional -- the payoff is very high if the game goes long.

> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Does not perform well against high-mobility heroes. Invoker has high damage output but low reliable control -- mobile heroes can dodge or outmanoeuvre his spell combinations. BKB is a hard counter: once activated, it blocks the majority of his spell damage and combos entirely.
> [2026-05-28]: REFRESHER PATTERN -- Refresher Orb is specifically purchased to work around BKB. First set of spells forces the enemy to pop BKB. Once BKB is activated the enemy is committed to it, and when the second set of spells (refreshed) lands either during or after BKB, they are unable to respond. Pattern: Combo 1 to burn BKB --> Combo 2 to kill. This is the primary answer to BKB-reliant cores.
