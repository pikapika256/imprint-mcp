> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Mid-game bully. QoP deals high magic damage consistently throughout the game and uses her mobility to apply constant pressure. Strongest in the mid-game window before enemies have full BKB uptime.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Kaya and Sange into Aghanim's Scepter. Kaya and Sange provides spell amplification, HP, and status resistance. Aghs upgrades her kit for increased teamfight output.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (FLEXIBLE) -- After core, items are match-dependent: BKB (survivability and spell delivery), Shiva's Guard (AOE slow + armour), Octarine Core (cooldown reduction + spell lifesteal), Bloodstone (sustain), Hex (single-target lockdown), Linken's Sphere (vs single-target control). All are standard options depending on what the game requires.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Magic damage identity means BKB is a hard counter. Teams with multiple BKB users significantly reduce QoP's effectiveness. Should be assessed against BKB availability in the enemy draft.
