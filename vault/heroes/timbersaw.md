> [2026-05-27]: TIER — Top pos 3 offlaner in 7.41c.
> [2026-05-27]: Magic damage + Timber Chain mobility fits the teamfight meta. Blink Dagger enables chain initiation.
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Pos 3 offlaner. Pure damage caster with high survivability.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Skill points in Reactive Armour give Timbersaw high physical damage mitigation and HP regen in lane, making him very difficult to kill or zone out. Can sustain through aggressive lane matchups and stay relevant even in tough lanes.

> [2026-05-28]: MID GAME -- Primary identity is high pure damage output through Whirling Death and Chakram. Pure damage bypasses magic resistance but not spell immunity. Provides constant area threat in fights once laning phase is complete.

> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS (BKB) -- Hero is effectively useless against BKB. Has no control spells to stop enemies activating BKB, and cannot prevent BKB users from right-clicking him down freely. Enemy cores with BKB negate his offensive threat almost entirely while he has no answer.

> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS (MOBILITY) -- No control spells in the kit. Struggles to deal with heroes that have built-in mobility or can dodge Chakram positioning. Cannot lock anyone down -- entirely reliant on enemies being in range and unable to disengage.

> [2026-05-28]: STRENGTH HERO SYNERGY -- Particularly strong against strength heroes. Whirling Death reduces the target's primary attribute on proc. For strength heroes this means losing Strength = losing HP, HP regen, and damage simultaneously. Can accelerate kill timings significantly against strength offlaners and carries.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Kaya and Sange is the core item. Provides spell damage amplification, HP, status resistance, and movement speed -- addresses most of Timbersaw's early needs in one item.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (AFTER CORE) -- Flexible caster item build after Kaya and Sange. Options include: Blink Dagger (positioning/initiation), BKB (survivability vs magic-heavy lineups), Lotus Orb (reflect + dispel), Shiva's Guard (armour + AOE slow), Bloodstone (sustain + spell lifesteal), Octarine Core (cooldown reduction + spell lifesteal), Hex (single target lockdown), Windwaker (save + silence). Build is match-dependent based on what survivability or utility is needed.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM NOTE -- Timbersaw very rarely auto-attacks during fights. All damage output comes from spells. On-attack effect items (e.g. Maelstrom, Hydra's Breath, any attack proc) are largely wasted on this hero. Item build should focus entirely on spell amplification, cooldown reduction, and survivability.
