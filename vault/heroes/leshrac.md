# Leshrac
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 2 mid.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- High AOE magic damage dealer. Stable laning phase. Deals enormous magic damage to multiple targets with Diabolic Edict and Lightning Storm. Bloodstone provides sustain -- Leshrac heals from spell damage dealt, making him hard to kill in extended fights when Bloodstone is active.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Struggles against high DPS heroes that can burst him down before he has time to proc Bloodstone healing. If killed quickly before his spells deal significant damage he loses his sustain window entirely.
> [2026-05-28]: ILLUSION COUNTER -- Hard counter to illusion-based heroes. Diabolic Edict AOE clears illusions efficiently -- particularly effective against Phantom Lancer, Naga Siren, and Terrorblade.
