## Player Associations

> [2026-05-28]: DOOM COUNTER — Ranged high damage output punishes Doom before he can close the gap. Windranger can kite Scorched Earth and stay outside AOE Doom range while dealing significant damage.
> [2026-05-28]: General pattern: ranged carries with high single-target damage output counter Doom's melee chase-and-zone style.
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Pos 1 carry. Strong laning hero with high single-target physical damage in the late game.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Good auto-attack harass combined with Powershot makes the laning phase strong. Windrun makes it difficult for opponents to apply pressure back; she can take trades favourably and disengage safely.

> [2026-05-28]: KIT -- Shackleshot provides single-target lockdown (tree/unit line-up dependent). Focus Fire dramatically increases attack speed on a single target. Windrun allows Windranger to stick to a fleeing target while avoiding right-click damage. Late-game identity is high single-target burst: Focus Fire + Shackleshot locks down and deletes one hero.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Maelstrom into Mjollnir for farming efficiency. BKB is a core defensive item to survive the burst window while applying Focus Fire. Dragon Lance is a core pickup as it builds into two situational upgrades -- Hurricane Pike (mobility/push-away active, kiting and disengage) or Hydra's Breath (% max health DoT + split shot, preferred vs high-HP heroes or when AOE right-click value is high). Choice is match-dependent. Daedalus is a core damage item in the build.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE/SITUATIONAL) -- After Daedalus, final items are typically defensive: Linken's Sphere (vs single-target spells/Doom), Manta Style (dispel + illusions), Satanic (sustain + lifesteal). All are viable depending on the enemy lineup. Blink Dagger is occasionally correct if Windranger needs to initiate rather than respond. Aghanim's Scepter may be viable but specifics are unclear -- do not recommend without further research.

> [2026-05-28]: DOOM MATCHUP -- Windranger is a strong counter to Doom. Ranged high damage output allows her to punish him before he closes the gap with Scorched Earth. Linken's Sphere also directly blocks Doom's ultimate if purchased.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Windranger and Muerta share a strong matchup into right-click focused carries: Ursa, Troll Warlord, Phantom Assassin. Ranged damage output + mobility (Windrun) avoids their right-click trades. Shackleshot + Focus Fire allows Windranger to delete these heroes in the lockdown window. Note: Muerta uses Silver Edge to apply Break on these heroes; Windranger less commonly builds Silver Edge.

> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played as position 4 support. When played pos 4, the hero is built around control spells rather than the ultimate / attack damage amplification. The carry-oriented ultimate is treated as a bonus rather than the primary identity. Provides lockdown and utility at lower farm levels without needing the full carry item build.
