# Keeper of the Light

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Flex across position 2 (mid), 4, and 5. Role assigned based on team needs.
> [2026-05-28]: SPIRIT FORM -- Ultimate places KOTL in Spirit Form, granting significant bonus cast range and upgraded abilities. Enables abuse of long-range item activations: Force Staff, Eul's Scepter, Hex all become available from a much greater distance. Fundamentally changes how the hero interacts with items.

> [2026-05-28]: SUPPORT IDENTITY (POS 4/5) -- Provides healing and mana regeneration to the team, enabling cores to sustain longer in fights and across the map. Spirit Form cast range bonus allows use of Force Staff, Eul's, and Hex at extended range. Holy Locket is a core item on the support build (amplifies all healing output).

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (SUPPORT) -- Holy Locket (core), Force Staff, Eul's Scepter of Divinity, Hex. Items chosen around the bonus cast range and healing amplification identity.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (MID) -- Spirit Vessel (over-time damage + healing reduction), Boots of Travel (global mobility + frequent Spirit Form usage), Octarine Core (cooldown reduction for more Spirit Form uptime). Scales into late-game items: Hex (lockdown), BKB (survivability for fighting), Aghanim's Scepter (kit upgrade). Becomes a strong teamfight presence with full items.
> [2026-05-28]: MID VS SUPPORT TIMING -- Same late-game item build is accessible at both roles but the timing is later as a support due to lower farm priority. Mid KOTL reaches the full build significantly faster.
