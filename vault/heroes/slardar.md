# Slardar

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Pos 2 or pos 3 (flex). Initiator and teamfight space creator.
> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played at position 2 or position 3. This flex potential is a primary reason for the high ban rate -- banning Slardar simplifies the draft by removing a hero that can slot into multiple roles without commitment.

> [2026-05-28]: DAMAGE AMP -- Corrosive Haze applies a strong armour reduction debuff. Enables a wider range of position 1 heroes to be viable by amplifying physical damage against the debuffed target. Changes how matchups work -- low-armour heroes become much more vulnerable, and heroes that otherwise could not trade efficiently against tanky targets suddenly become viable.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Blink Dagger and BKB. Blink enables initiation; BKB allows Slardar to survive in the middle of fights while applying Corrosive Haze.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Strong in lane against melee heroes. In-game, strong against low-armour heroes where Corrosive Haze provides maximum value.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 109 bans but only 34.4% win rate when picked (28% as pos 3). Poor win rate when played suggests the hero is situational or requires a very specific draft to perform. Banned more as a draft-simplification tool and because of the damage amp enabling specific pos 1 combinations than because of standalone strength.
