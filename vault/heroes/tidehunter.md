> [2026-05-27]: TIER — Top pos 3 offlaner in 7.41c.
> [2026-05-27]: Ravage is one of the best Rubick steal targets in the game — Tidehunter + Rubick in the same game is a dangerous combo for the enemy.
> [2026-05-27]: Kraken Shell shrug-off reduces effectiveness of caster damage. Natural frontliner.
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Pos 3 offlaner. Control and survivability hero. Low damage output but exceptional teamfight presence.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Kraken Shell provides passive damage block against physical attacks. Anchor Smash applies a damage reduction debuff on nearby enemies, reducing their right-click output. Combined, these two abilities make Tidehunter extremely difficult to kill with right-click damage in lane and in fights -- similar survivability profile to Timbersaw's Reactive Armour, but oriented around physical damage specifically.

> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Not a damage hero. Primary value is control and survivability. Ravage is one of the best teamfight ultimates in the game -- large AOE stun that can hit the entire enemy team in the right positioning. Supports and enables the rest of the team rather than dealing damage himself.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Consecrated Wraps is the core first item. After Wraps, next item is either Blink Dagger or Aghanim's Scepter depending on the game state.

> [2026-05-28]: AGHS + SHARD -- Aghanim's Shard combined with Blink Dagger provides a low-cooldown control pattern for dealing with mobile heroes. The Shard gives Tidehunter a reliable way to lock down targets between Ravage cooldowns.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (AFTER CORE) -- After Wraps + Blink/Aghs, remaining items are flexible and game-dependent. Options include: Shiva's Guard (armour + AOE slow), Hex (single-target lockdown), BKB (survivability vs magic damage), Lotus Orb (reflect + dispel), Refresher Orb (double Ravage). No fixed order -- all match-dependent.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Counters melee heroes reliant on right-click damage. Kraken Shell blocks physical hits and Anchor Smash reduces their damage output. Effectively neutralises right-click dependent melee cores in extended fights.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (BAD) -- Countered by Break (removes Kraken Shell passive damage block, removing his physical survivability) and magic damage (bypasses Kraken Shell entirely). Both approaches negate his core defensive identity.

> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERING THE COUNTERS -- BKB purchased to mitigate magic damage lineups. Mage Slayer reduces incoming magic damage to reduce the effectiveness of magic-heavy counter drafts. Build either depending on which counter is more relevant.
