# Luna

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 1 carry. Stable laning hero and hyper farmer.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Farms faster than most carries but gets outscaled by many of them in the late game. The strategy is to leverage farm speed into an early item advantage and use that window to take objectives and fights before the game goes too long.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Manta Style, Satanic, Butterfly, BKB. Target an Aegis timing at 25-30 minutes with this core built or near-complete. The Aegis + full item core is the power window to force fights and take Roshan/high ground.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP CONCERNS -- Generally few hard counters. Main weakness is illusion-based heroes and heroes that create illusions of Luna specifically:
> - Dark Seer (Ion Shell placed on a Luna illusion deals reflected damage)
> - Grimstroke Aghanim's Scepter (creates a phantom of Luna that deals her damage against herself)
> - Shadow Demon (Disruption creates Luna illusions)
> These matchups are unfavourable but not unplayable -- they slow down Luna's fighting and require more care.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 56.2% win rate on 16 picks at pos 1, 18 bans. Solid performer at low volume. Low ban rate relative to performance -- may be undervalued as a ban target.
