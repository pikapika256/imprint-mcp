# Naga Siren
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 1 carry (primary). Increasingly used as a support for vision and teamfight Song of the Siren.
> [2026-05-28]: POS 1 IDENTITY -- Hyper farmer and illusion carry. Farms very quickly. Against lineups with no AOE stuns or AOE damage, illusions become impossible to deal with. Laning phase is weak -- needs protection or lane prioritisation.
> [2026-05-28]: SUPPORT IDENTITY -- Emerging support pick. Illusions provide extensive map vision without revealing Naga Siren herself. Song of the Siren provides a global teamfight pause, enabling setup for allies or disengaging from bad fights. Usually a last-pick option to exploit specific draft gaps.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 3 picks (100% WR), 4 bans. Low sample but perfect win rate suggests highly targeted picks.
