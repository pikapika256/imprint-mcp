> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Tanky strength hero that scales into significant right-click damage. Quill Spray stacks scale with each proc. Bristleback passive reduces damage taken from behind/sides, countering heroes that cannot reposition to face him or chase him down.

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Counters heroes that cannot reposition to face Bristleback or cannot escape his chase.
