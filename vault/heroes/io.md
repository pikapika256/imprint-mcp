# Io
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 4 or 5 support.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Amplification support. Tethers to an ally providing: healing, movement speed boost, attack speed, and spell amplification. Makes the tethered core significantly stronger in lane and in fights. Global presence via Relocate ultimate.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- No fixed item timing. Can be played greedily with Aghanim's Scepter for high personal damage output, or purely as a utility support (Glimmer Cape, Holy Locket, etc.).
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Vulnerable to heroes that can find and kill Io directly -- removing Io removes all the amplification for the tethered ally. Also vulnerable to large AOE while tethered to an ally, as Io is stationary and easy to catch in AOE damage.
> [2026-05-28]: COMFORT BAN -- Noted as a comfort ban for certain teams (see patches/7.41.md). Ignored entirely against teams that do not play Io.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 44.4% win rate on 9 picks, 42 bans. Almost always banned due to global/amplification threat despite low pick count.
