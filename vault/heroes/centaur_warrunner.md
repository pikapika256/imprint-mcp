# Centaur Warrunner
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 3 offlaner.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Extremely survivable strength hero with a strong stun. Stampede repositions the entire team in fights -- enables ally escapes, chases, or sudden initiation. Fights by either Blinking in or becoming so durable that walking straight into the enemy is viable.
> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Hard counter to Windranger (Double Edge + tankiness negates her Shackleshot/Focus Fire pattern). Generally strong against ranged carries that deal continuous damage but struggle against a durable target that closes the gap. Also good against heroes with big teamfight spells where Stampede enables the team to disengage before the spell lands.
