# Tusk

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 4 or 5 support.
> [2026-05-28]: LANE IDENTITY -- Pairs well with ranged heroes to abuse Tag Team. Tag Team increases damage taken by a target Tusk hits, creating high kill threat in lane when the ranged support can follow up on the amplified damage. Good gap closing in lane via Shard and Snowball -- can initiate on enemies or close distance for kill attempts early.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Blink Dagger into Force Staff or Eul's Scepter. Blink enables range to initiate or save; Force Staff/Eul's provide further mobility and utility.

> [2026-05-28]: SAVE SUPPORT -- Primary save mechanic is Snowball: picks up nearby allies to carry them away from danger. Blink Dagger enables getting into range quickly to execute the save. Pairs the save utility with fight initiation on the same ability.

> [2026-05-28]: BKB-PIERCING ULTIMATE -- Walrus Punch is BKB-piercing. Reliable single-target lockdown in the mid to late game regardless of BKB status. Provides consistent pick-off potential against BKB-reliant heroes.

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 60.0% win rate on 15 picks at pos 5, 15 bans. Strong performer at low volume. Even ban rate suggests moderate priority.
