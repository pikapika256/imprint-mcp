> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (20-80): unplayable. Viper (30-70): highly unfavourable. Sniper (40-60): unfavourable. Creep drag viable for all three (melee hero).
> [2026-05-28]: SLIGHT DISADVANTAGE (LANE) -- Pangolier (45-55).
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Tiny (50-50), Primal Beast (50-50), Sand King (50-50).
> [2026-05-28]: LANING CHEESE -- Melee hero. Creep drag is the standard response to Huskar, Viper, and Sniper matchups.

> [2026-05-28]: WAVE CUT (TIER 2) -- Can execute wave cutting behind the enemy tower in unfavourable matchups, but less effectively than Sand King/Primal Beast. Typically improves a bad matchup to around 40-60 rather than 50-50. Still valuable vs hard counters like Huskar and Viper.

> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Fight-heavy core. High mobility and survivability allow Earth Spirit to be in the middle of fights consistently. Can lack raw damage output but compensates with persistent soft control -- slows, silences, and a low cooldown stun via Rolling Boulder. Designed to stay in fights longer than most, applying continuous disruption.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Spirit Vessel and BKB are the core items. Spirit Vessel provides over-time damage and healing reduction, synergising with Earth Spirit's sustained presence in fights. BKB enables him to sit in the middle of fights without being instantly disabled, which is required to get full value from his kit.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (FLEXIBLE) -- After core, build varies by game: Octarine Core (cooldown reduction for more Rolling Boulder stuns), Shiva's Guard (armour + AOE slow), Bloodstone (sustain), Aghanim's Scepter (kit upgrades), Refresher Orb (double fight presence), Kaya and Sange (spell amp + HP + status resistance).
