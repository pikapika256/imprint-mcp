> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only. Huskar is one of the most dominant laning heroes in the pool -- nearly unbeatable by melee mids, only countered by ranged heroes that can kite him.
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERS HUSKAR (LANE) -- Sniper (35-65 huskar), Viper (35-65 huskar): ranged heroes that can outrange and kite Huskar's aggression. These are Huskar's only poor matchups in the pool.
> [2026-05-28]: DOMINATES (LANE) -- Tiny (80-20), Sand King (80-20): near-unplayable for the opponent. Primal Beast (70-30): heavily favoured. Pangolier (65-35): clearly favoured. All Spirit heroes and most melee mids fall into the 20-80 or similar range.
> [2026-05-28]: DRAFT NOTE -- Huskar is a lane-dominant pick that forces the enemy to either pick a ranged mid counter or concede the mid lane entirely. Melee mid heroes should not be drafted into Huskar without a plan (e.g. creep drag or jungling).

> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Tempo bully. Leverages dominant laning phase to immediately pressure objectives. The goal is to translate lane advantage into tower kills and map control before the enemy can stabilise. Armlet of Mordiggian is the key timing -- once completed, Huskar looks to fight and take objectives.
> [2026-05-28]: SURVIVABILITY PRIORITY -- Once a lead is generated from lane, not dying is paramount. Huskar's identity is staying alive through high sustain while right-clicking enemies down. The build focuses heavily on survivability to protect the lead.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (STANDARD) -- Armlet of Mordiggian (tempo timing), Blink Dagger (gap close/initiation), Sange and Yasha (HP + status resistance + movement speed), BKB (spell immunity for safe right-clicking), Satanic (lifesteal + sustain), Assault Cuirass (attack speed + armour).
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (SITUATIONAL) -- Aghanim's Scepter can be added for extra control options.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Right-click damage and high regeneration/sustain are the core of the hero. Relies on outlasting enemies in prolonged fights through lifesteal and HP regen.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTER -- Ancient Apparition is a hard counter. Ice Blast prevents all regeneration and healing on the target -- directly nullifies Huskar's sustain identity. A hero with no sustain is far easier to kill. Always worth considering as a ban or counter-pick when Huskar is in the draft.
