> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Pos 1 carry. Playstyle and item build closely mirrors Windranger (see windranger.md for full build notes).

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Follows the same core as Windranger: Maelstrom/Mjollnir into BKB, Dragon Lance into Hurricane Pike or situational upgrade, Daedalus. Late-game defensive items (Linken's Sphere, Manta Style, Satanic) are all viable.
> [2026-05-28]: SILVER EDGE -- Silver Edge is a core item for Muerta (not standard for Windranger). Applies Break, removing enemy passive abilities. This is the defining difference in item builds between the two heroes. Particularly effective against heroes whose identity relies on passives (see MATCHUP section).

> [2026-05-28]: MATCHUP (GOOD) -- Both Muerta and Windranger counter right-click focused carries whose strength is passive-dependent or relies on raw right-click damage output: Ursa (Fury Swipes stacks), Phantom Assassin (Coup de Grace crit + Blur evasion), Troll Warlord (Battle Trance, high attack speed). Silver Edge removes these passives; Windrun/Muerta mobility + ranged damage avoids their right-click trades. These are strong picks to target-pick against those heroes.

> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played as position 4 support. When played pos 4, the hero is built around control spells rather than the ultimate / attack damage amplification. The carry-oriented ultimate is treated as a bonus rather than the primary identity. Provides lockdown and utility at lower farm levels without needing the full carry item build.
