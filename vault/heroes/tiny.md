> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (20-80), Sniper (35-65), Viper (35-65): heavily unfavourable. Melee hero -- creep drag is the standard response.
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Pangolier (50-50), Primal Beast (50-50), Sand King (50-50). Even vs most other melee mids.
> [2026-05-28]: LANING CHEESE -- Melee hero. Creep drag applicable vs Huskar, Sniper, Viper.

> [2026-05-28]: WAVE CUT (TIER 2) -- Can execute wave cutting behind the enemy tower in unfavourable matchups, but less effectively than Sand King/Primal Beast. Typically improves a bad matchup to around 40-60 rather than 50-50. Still valuable vs hard counters like Huskar and Viper.

> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Active early mid laner who looks to use power spikes before spell damage begins to fall off in the late game. Needs to generate advantages and take objectives early before transitioning.
> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played as position 4 playing for magic damage and control rather than as a damage-focused mid. Item build adjusts accordingly (see pos 4 path below).
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CASTER / POS 4 PATH) -- Blink Dagger first. Then flexible from: Phylactery (bonus damage proc on spells, extends early spell burst), Eul's Scepter (setup + self-save), Octarine Core (cooldown reduction), BKB (survivability), Dagon (burst damage), Hex (single-target lockdown). Played for magic damage and control.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (RIGHT-CLICK / POS 1 PATH) -- Currently also played as a position 1 carry. After Blink Dagger, pivots into a right-click build: Echo Sabre into Harpoon (pull-in lockdown), Shadow Blade (escape/initiation), BKB (spell immunity for right-clicking), Assault Cuirass (attack speed + armour), Daedalus (critical strike damage). Leverages Tree Grab to amplify right-click damage significantly in the late game.
