# Lion

> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 4 support. High control hero.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- Lots of hard control (Earth Spike stun, Hex, Mana Drain, Finger of Death). Provides reliable lockdown making him an answer to slippery mid heroes -- particularly effective against Spirit heroes and Puck which are dominant in the current meta. When the mid lane pool is Spirit/Puck-heavy, Lion is a strong support answer.

> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Blink Dagger and Force Staff. Blink enables initiation to land control spells; Force Staff provides save and further positioning utility.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (PATHS) -- After core, two directions: utility items to support the team further, or Shadow Blade + BKB to play to scale (Shadow Blade provides an initiation vector, BKB enables survival to deliver Finger of Death in late-game teamfights).

> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 63.9% win rate on 36 picks at pos 4, 9 bans. Strong performer and relatively low ban rate -- potentially undervalued given the win rate. Good target pick when Spirit/Puck mid heroes are expected.
