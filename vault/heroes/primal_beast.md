> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (30-70), Viper (35-65): heavily unfavourable. Creep drag applicable (melee hero).
> [2026-05-28]: UNFAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Storm Spirit (45-55), Ember Spirit (45-55), Void Spirit (45-55), Queen of Pain (45-55).
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Puck (50-50), Invoker (50-50), Snapfire (50-50), Earth Spirit (50-50), Sniper (50-50), Tiny (50-50), Pangolier (50-50), Sand King (50-50).

> [2026-05-28]: WAVE CUT (TIER 1) -- Sand King and Primal Beast are the primary wave-cutting melee mids. Strong AOE damage clears the wave behind the enemy tower efficiently, converting even 20-80 matchups (Huskar/Viper) into approximately 50-50 or better.

> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played as position 3 offlaner with the same build and playstyle.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Initiator and disruptive teamfighter. Charges into fights, disrupts enemy positioning, and deals damage through movement-based abilities. Relies on being able to move freely -- easy to kite if movement is restricted.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Blink Dagger and BKB are the core. Blink enables initiation; BKB is required to prevent being immediately disabled on entry -- without BKB Primal Beast is trivially kited and loses the ability to deal damage.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (STANDARD) -- After core: Aghanim's Scepter (kit upgrades), Refresher Orb (double fight presence), Shiva's Guard (AOE slow + armour).
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE) -- Flexible last items: Octarine Core (cooldown reduction), Hex (single-target lockdown), Kaya and Sange (spell amp + HP + status resistance), Linken's Sphere (survivability vs single-target control).
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Countered by BKB-piercing disables. Relies on movement to deal damage; BKB-piercing control completely shuts down his fighting pattern.
> [2026-05-28]: LINKEN'S SPHERE VS PRIMAL BEAST -- Linken's Sphere is a core counter item against Primal Beast. His ultimate (Pulverize -- the grab/slam) is what makes him disruptive, and Linken's blocks it on a single target. High priority item purchase for carries or key heroes facing Primal Beast.
