> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only. Laning advantages snowball significantly and are the most important phase for Storm Spirit given his snowball potential.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (20-80): unplayable after level 3, Storm must retreat to jungle. Viper (30-70): similar dynamic, highly unfavourable.
> [2026-05-28]: UNFAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Sniper (35-65): outranged. Snapfire (40-60): more raw damage. Queen of Pain (40-60): outranged.
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Invoker (50-50). Pangolier (50-50).
> [2026-05-28]: FAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Tiny (55-45), Primal Beast (55-45), Sand King (55-45), Ember Spirit (55-45), Void Spirit (60-40), Earth Spirit (60-40). Skill-based matchups vs other mobility heroes where Storm's burst and ball lightning trades are effective.
> [2026-05-28]: SLIGHT DISADVANTAGE (LANE) -- Puck (45-55): Puck slightly favoured.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING CHEESE -- Against hard counter matchups (Huskar, Viper) and matchups outside 40-60, Spirit heroes can drag creeps behind the enemy tower to deny the enemy lane cs and neutralise the bad matchup. Effectively makes unwinnable lanes closer to 50-50. Standard response to unfavourable mid pairings.


> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Can make active moves early by roaming or countering enemy rotations, but is reliant on using active runes to enable this aggression. Not a hero that forces fights on his own early -- works best reacting to or capitalising on rune timings.
> [2026-05-28]: SCALING -- Scales well into the late game. Storm Spirit gets stronger as items come online. Not a hero that needs to end early.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Kaya is the core first item. Amplifies spell damage and mana cost reduction, enabling more frequent Ball Lightning usage.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (SITUATIONAL) -- Orchid Malevolence is a strong situational pickup against heroes that rely on spells to escape or fight back. Particularly effective vs Puck (silence prevents Phase Shift/Illusory Orb) and Ember Spirit (silence stops Sleight of Fist/Fire Remnant escape).
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE GAME -- AOE) -- Aghanim's Scepter + Refresher Orb: AOE teamfight build. Aghs upgrades Overload to deal damage in an AOE, Refresher enables double ultimate usage for sustained teamfight presence.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE GAME -- BURST) -- Parasma + Hex: single target burst build. Parasma amplifies spell damage on a single target, Hex provides hard lockdown to land the full combo. Designed to delete one hero per fight.
> [2026-05-28]: BKB -- Usually required. BKB enables Ball Lightning's hyper mobility by granting spell immunity during the channel, preventing enemies from interrupting or disabling Storm while he repositions or initiates.
