# Templar Assassin
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 1 carry.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- High burst physical damage carry. Refraction charges absorb incoming damage instances. Relies on Refraction charges for survivability -- DoT damage (poison, burn, over-time effects) burns through charges quickly as each tick removes a charge.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Weak to heroes that can gap close and stick on her (melee initiators) and to DoT damage sources that drain Refraction charges without requiring direct engagement. Also weak to heroes that provide vision -- Psi Blades only work from the front so awareness of position is crucial.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 50.0% win rate on 4 picks, 3 bans. Very low volume.
