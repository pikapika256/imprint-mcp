> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only. Viper is one of the most dominant laning heroes in the pool alongside Huskar -- hard counters melee mids and is difficult to play against ranged heroes too.
> [2026-05-28]: DOMINATES (LANE) -- Sand King (70-30), Tiny (65-35), Pangolier (65-35), Primal Beast (65-35). All melee Spirit heroes fall heavily to Viper. Near-unbeatable vs most melee mids.
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERED BY (LANE) -- Sniper (35-65): Sniper outranges and kites. Huskar (35-65): Huskar's burn damage and aggression counters Viper.
> [2026-05-28]: DRAFT NOTE -- Along with Huskar, Viper is a primary lane-forcing pick. Drafting either forces the enemy team to answer with a ranged mid or accept a very difficult laning phase.

> [2026-05-28]: MOBILITY -- Low mobility. No built-in escape or gap-closing spells. Boots of Travel is a standard early purchase to compensate, providing global mobility that offsets the lack of natural movement options.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Can develop into two distinct roles after laning: frontline tank or ranged right-click carry. Path is chosen based on draft needs and game state.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (SHARED) -- Boots of Travel first. Hurricane Pike is the preferred Dragon Lance upgrade over Hydra's Breath -- the push-away active provides a defensive option for an otherwise immobile hero.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (TANK PATH) -- Consecrated Wraps, Aghanim's Scepter, Shiva's Guard form the frontline core. Late-game spellcaster items to extend: Bloodstone, Hex, Blink Dagger, Kaya and Sange, BKB.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (RIGHT-CLICK PATH) -- Physical damage carry build. Items are match-dependent from: Butterfly (evasion + attack speed), Satanic (lifesteal), Daedalus (critical strike), Shadow Blade (escape/initiation), BKB (spell immunity), Eye of Skadi (slow + stat reduction), Manta Style (dispel + illusions).
