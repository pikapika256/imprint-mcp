# Enigma
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 3 offlaner (primarily).
> [2026-05-28]: LANING -- Oppressive lane against teams with no AOE spells to clear Eidolons. Hard countered in lane by Gyrocopter (AOE clears Eidolons easily).
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD -- Blink Dagger + BKB is the standard core for Black Hole initiation. BKB Black Hole is a very strong teamfight spell but requires Blink to land reliably. Mobile heroes (Puck, Ember Spirit, Storm Spirit) can make it difficult to land Blink and set up Black Hole.
> [2026-05-28]: ALTERNATIVE BUILD -- If BKB Black Hole is not reliable (too many mobile heroes, silences), can pivot to aura items and objective taking (Vladmir Offering, Assault Cuirass, etc.) using Eidolons to push.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 1 pick (100% WR), 14 bans. Almost always banned due to Black Hole threat.
