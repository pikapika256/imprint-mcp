> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Highly mobile hero that split pushes more than most mid heroes. Uses mobility to apply lane pressure across the map while remaining active on power runes. Very active playstyle requiring constant decision-making.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Witch Blade first item, followed by Blink Dagger. Witch Blade provides attack slow + bonus damage for wave clear and last hitting. Blink Dagger enables initiation and escape.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (DAMAGE PATH) -- After Witch Blade + Blink, build either Parasma (single target burst build) or Octarine Core (spell-focused build with cooldown reduction). Choice is match-dependent.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (SURVIVABILITY) -- Items are chosen to patch specific vulnerabilities: Eul's Scepter against silences (self-purge), Linken's Sphere against single-target control spells, Aeon Disk against instant stuns that Linken's does not block (e.g. AOE stuns, some item stuns). These are matchup-dependent additions rather than a fixed slot.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE) -- Final items vary widely. Options include Kaya and Sange, Bloodstone, Shiva's Guard, Dagon depending on what the game requires.
> [2026-05-28]: PRE-BKB ROLE -- Primary window of impact. Uses Dream Coil to lock down high-mobility targets before they purchase BKB. Being active on power runes is core to enabling early fight participation. Dream Coil forces enemies to stand still or take massive damage -- very effective before BKB counters it.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-BKB ROLE -- Transitions to poking and harassing to force enemies to pop BKB. Once BKB is activated and expires, Puck uses Dream Coil immediately to catch and control the target in the window before BKB comes back off cooldown. Playstyle revolves around baiting and timing around BKB cooldowns.
