> [2026-05-28]: MID POOL -- Included in the 7.41c mid lane matchup pool.

> [2026-05-28]: LANING NOTE -- All matchup splits are laning phase only.
> [2026-05-28]: HARD COUNTERS (LANE) -- Huskar (35-65), Viper (35-65): heavily unfavourable. Creep drag applicable (melee hero).
> [2026-05-28]: FAVOURABLE (LANE) -- Sniper (55-45), Snapfire (55-45), Earth Spirit (55-45), Puck (60-40): notably Pangolier performs well into ranged heroes that other melee mids struggle against.
> [2026-05-28]: EVEN (LANE) -- Primal Beast (50-50), Sand King (50-50), Tiny (50-50), Void Spirit (50-50).

> [2026-05-28]: WAVE CUT (TIER 2) -- Can execute wave cutting behind the enemy tower in unfavourable matchups, but less effectively than Sand King/Primal Beast. Typically improves a bad matchup to around 40-60 rather than 50-50. Still valuable vs hard counters like Huskar and Viper.

> [2026-05-28]: FLEX -- Can be played as position 3 offlaner with a similar item build and playstyle. Mid and pos 3 roles are largely interchangeable for Pangolier.
> [2026-05-28]: POST-LANE ROLE -- Disruptive teamfighter. Uses Rolling Thunder and mobility to create chaos in fights, disabling multiple heroes and enabling follow-up from teammates.
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (CORE) -- Diffusal Blade and Mage Slayer are the early core. Diffusal provides mana burn and slow; Mage Slayer reduces the magic damage incoming, addressing his vulnerability to burst. Then: Blink Dagger (initiation), Aghanim's Shard (improved Rolling Thunder), Aghanim's Scepter (kit upgrade), Octarine Core (cooldown reduction), Shiva's Guard (AOE slow + armour).
> [2026-05-28]: ITEM BUILD (LATE) -- Refresher Orb for double Rolling Thunder and extended fight presence, or Abyssal Blade/Basher to provide BKB-piercing lockdown on a single target.
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERS (SUPPORT) -- Bane (Fiend's Grip is BKB-piercing and interrupts Rolling Thunder), Grimstroke (BKB-piercing control).
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTERS (CORE) -- Windranger (Shackleshot + Focus Fire can delete Pangolier in the lockdown window), Faceless Void (Chronosphere locks Pangolier in place completely, BKB-piercing).
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Countered by BKB-piercing damage and control. Heroes and items that bypass spell immunity significantly reduce Pangolier's ability to disrupt fights.
