# Gyrocopter
> [2026-05-28]: ROLE -- Position 1 carry.
> [2026-05-28]: IDENTITY -- AOE damage ranged carry. Strong at all points in the game -- meaningful laning presence, strong mid-game fighting, and scales into late game. Rocket Barrage and Flak Cannon provide consistent AOE damage in fights.
> [2026-05-28]: WEAKNESS -- Vulnerable to heroes that want to man-fight -- closing the gap and right-clicking Gyrocopter is his primary weakness as a ranged carry without strong escape or defensive tools.
> [2026-05-28]: COUNTER ROLE -- Hard counters Enigma (AOE clears Eidolons, removing lane dominance) and Lycan (AOE clears wolves and controlled creep). Worth considering as a counter-pick when either hero is expected.
> [2026-05-28]: META (DreamLeague S29) -- 50.0% win rate on 2 picks, 4 bans. Very low sample. Ban rate reflects counter-pick value against specific heroes.
