# Countering the Blink/Force Staff Caster Meta (7.41c)

> [2026-05-27]: DEBUFF IMMUNITY — BKB lets cores fight through casters without being disabled. Prioritise fast BKB timings on carries vs this meta.
> [2026-05-27]: BACKLINE CATCHERS — Spirit heroes and Puck can dive and kill casters before they set up. Punishes poor caster positioning.
> [2026-05-27]: VISION ADVANTAGE — Better wards/smoke lets your team land spells first before enemy uses Blink/Force Staff. Vision fights win against this archetype.
> [2026-05-27]: The counter lives in the carry/mid picks and macro vision control — not necessarily in picking a counter support.
