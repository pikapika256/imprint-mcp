## Notes

> [2026-05-27]: Strongest pos 4 heroes in 7.41c: Phoenix, Rubick, Clockwerk, Techies, Hoodwink.
> [2026-05-27]: The position 4 is one of the most influential roles this patch. Prioritise securing a top-tier pos 4 hero in draft.
> [2026-05-28]: CORRECTION — Not all top pos 4 heroes utilise Blink/Force Staff equally. Phoenix is the exception — Icarus Dive provides built-in mobility so Blink Dagger is redundant on him. Rubick, Clockwerk, Techies, and Hoodwink all benefit from Blink/Force Staff but Phoenix does not.
> [2026-05-27]: Glimmer Cape and Solar Crest are weak this patch — traditional pos 4 item paths relying on these are sub-optimal.

> [2026-05-27]: SAVE SUPPORT ARCHETYPE strong in pos 4 — Rubick exemplifies this. Heroes that can prevent ally deaths (Force Staff, repositioning tools, heals with mobility) are highly valued alongside the Blink/Force Staff damage dealers.
> [2026-05-27]: Dual-function pos 4 heroes (engage OR save depending on situation) are particularly hard to draft against.

> [2026-05-27]: CLOCKWERK NICHE — Vision and space creation. Different to initiator role — controls fight geometry rather than opening fights outright.
> [2026-05-27]: TECHIES NICHE — Map control via mine vision. Reactive Taser disarm pre-BKB is a strong save tool. Effectiveness declines post-BKB on enemy cores — time value accordingly.
> [2026-05-27]: HOODWINK NICHE — Semi-carry pos 4 trajectory. Wave push income accelerates item timings. Scurry + Force Staff has higher mechanical ceiling than other pos 4 Force Staff users.
