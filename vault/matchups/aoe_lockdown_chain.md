# AOE Lockdown Chain — Chasm Stone Meta Pattern

> [2026-05-27]: Pattern: Hero with Chasm Stone (+40 AOE) → Gleipnir upgrade = stacked AOE lockdown. Bushwhack (Hoodwink) + Gleipnir root is the primary example in 7.41c.
> [2026-05-27]: Counter-play: Linken's Sphere blocks Gleipnir root on one hero. Spread positioning reduces multi-hero hit rate on Bushwhack.
> [2026-05-27]: Draft signal: if enemy picks Hoodwink + Chasm Stone path, consider Linken's Sphere on the most vulnerable hero (usually carry).

> [2026-05-28]: CONFIRMED — Gleipnir root IS blocked by Linken's Sphere. Linken's is the primary item counter to Gleipnir. Draft Linken's on the carry if enemy is running Hoodwink + Gleipnir lockdown chain.

> [2026-05-28]: CONFIRMED — Gleipnir does NOT go through BKB. BKB + Linken's are the two main counters: BKB = team-wide fight-through, Linken's = single-target block. Draft at least one of these on carry vs Hoodwink/Gleipnir.
