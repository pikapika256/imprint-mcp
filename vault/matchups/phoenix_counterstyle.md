# Countering Phoenix — Draft Framework

> [2026-05-27]: Phoenix has long early cooldowns — lane bullies that are NOT susceptible to his damage when his spells are online can exploit this window.
> [2026-05-27]: Mid/late game Phoenix requires a draft-level answer, not just in-game adaptation. Counter picks must be selected before the game.
> [2026-05-27]: Reliable Supernova interrupt required in the lineup. Heroes with reliable interrupt tools (stuns, silences that apply quickly) are valuable against Phoenix drafts.
> [2026-05-27]: Avoid picking heroes vulnerable to Fire Spirits damage (sustained magic damage). Favour heroes with magic resistance or burst that kills before Sun Ray heals.
