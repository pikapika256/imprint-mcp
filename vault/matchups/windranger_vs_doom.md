# Windranger vs Doom

> [2026-05-28]: Windranger counters Doom. Ranged high damage output punishes Doom from distance before he can close with Scorched Earth.
> [2026-05-28]: Windranger can maintain distance and deal damage outside AOE Doom range. Doom's weakness to ranged heroes is a general pattern — not specific to Windranger only.
> [2026-05-28]: Doom player must account for Linken's on Windranger if she builds it — prevents single-target Doom. Doom's answer is Aghanims AOE build, which is then countered by Force Staff + BKB to disengage.
