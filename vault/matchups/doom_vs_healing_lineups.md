# Doom vs Healing/Sustain Lineups

> [2026-05-28]: Doom ability silences AND prevents healing on target. Strong counter to heroes/lineups built around healing (Lifestealer passive, Dazzle Shallow Grave + heals, Omniknight Purification, Wraith King reincarnation heal, Necrophos).
> [2026-05-28]: Draft Doom when enemy lineup relies on healing through fights rather than burst damage.
> [2026-05-28]: Doom vs mobility heroes: silence removes mobility spells from the target for the Doom duration.
